A Statistical Analysis of Top Marvel Rivals Players

Alwaid and I went through the current (2024-12-12) top 100 players in Marvel Rivals whose profiles were public, so 130 in total including the non-public ones, and analysed the statistics for their top 3 heroes by play time. Below are the results.

Be aware that there are some extreme outliers that are caused by OCR (optical character recognition) mistakes. We didn’t make an effort to thoroughly clean the data up as these should be relatively-obvious.
